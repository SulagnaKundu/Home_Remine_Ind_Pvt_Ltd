How to Recycle ?

1. Contact Us.

We will schedule a particular date and time for pickup.

2. Pack the items.

You should pack the items in a regular bag and keep them ready for recycling.

3. Wait for the bags to be collected.

We will collect the bag from your doorstep on the scheduled date and time.
